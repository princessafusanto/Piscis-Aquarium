About Us

Piscis Aquarium is a website that shows many kind of marine animals. We built this website with purpose to be the one of educational content for kids as our target and also adults.

By accessing our website, we hope the kids will get interested with marine animals and realise the sea creatures are as beautiful and amazing as land animal they often to see at zoo.
